About us

Who We Are

At Mineofy, we are a team of passionate engineers, data scientists, and industry experts dedicated to transforming the mining sector. With years of experience and a deep understanding of the challenges faced by mining companies, we leverage advanced drone technology and artificial intelligence to deliver unparalleled 3D modeling services.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower mining companies with the insights and tools they need to plan and operate more efficiently. By providing accurate and detailed 3D models, we aim to revolutionize mine planning and contribute to the sustainable and safe extraction of resources.

Mineofy Services

Drone-Based 3D Modeling

Using state-of-the-art drones, we capture high-resolution aerial images of your mining site. Our drones are equipped with the latest sensors and cameras, ensuring precise and comprehensive data collection.

AI-Driven Data Analysis

Our proprietary AI algorithms analyze the collected data to create detailed and accurate 3D models. This process not only saves time but also enhances the accuracy and reliability of the models.

Mine Planning Solutions

We provide comprehensive mine planning solutions, including volume calculations, slope stability analysis, and infrastructure planning. Our models help you visualize the mine layout, identify potential issues, and plan for efficient resource extraction.

Safety and Compliance

Our 3D models aid in monitoring and ensuring compliance with safety regulations. By identifying potential hazards and assessing environmental impact, we help you maintain a safe and compliant operation.
